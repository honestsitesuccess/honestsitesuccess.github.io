See more of our photo galleries:

  • Silver City Municipal Court
    The Town of Silver City's Municipal Court, open from 8 am to 4 pm, is at 215 North Bullard, in the Historic Downtown District. Photo by Silver City Police Chief Ed Reynolds.
  • Sitting on the corner of Bullard and Broadway in the Historic Downtown, Town Hall houses most administrative functions for Silver City as well as utility billing offices and payment counters. Photo by Silver City Police Chief Ed Reynolds.
  • Silver City's Public Safety Building houses the Silver City Police Department and one of two fire department station houses. Photo by Silver City Police Chief Ed Reynolds.
  • Find information on things to do, places to go and events for year-round fun at the Murray Ryan Visitor Center, 201 N. Hudson Street. Photo by Silver City Police Chief Ed Reynolds.
  • The Silver City Museum creates opportunities for residents and visitors to explore, understand, and celebrate the rich and diverse cultural heritage of southwestern New Mexico. Photo by Silver City Police Chief Ed Reynolds.
  • Silver City's historic water plant is being restored by civic-minded groups intent on preserving the Town's landmarks


Welcome to Silver City!

Silver City's strength and success come through the work of the many residents who take time to participate in their community. There's no better way to participate and shape Silver City's direction than as a member of one of the Town's boards or commissions. I invite you to learn more, and help make Silver City's future even brighter!
